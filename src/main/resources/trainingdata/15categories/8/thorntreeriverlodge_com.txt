We invite you to discover Victoria Falls Zambia from this tranquil setting... Created in a comfortable safari style Thorntree Lodge is a relaxed home away from home. Informal comfort in a natural and beautiful setting is completed with delicious al fresco meals that are taken within different locations of the lodge each day as the weather permits. The Mosi oa Tunya park stretches along the banks of the Zambezi River from Livingstone town heading upstream towards Botswana. Thorntree lies within the park about 12 minutes drive from the centre of Livingstone. This makes the sites of Livingstone, which include the world famous Victoria Falls, within easy reach yet guests can retreat to the lodge for peaceful relaxation. Within the grounds of Thorntree Lodge, a herd of domesticated orphaned elephants are housed.
