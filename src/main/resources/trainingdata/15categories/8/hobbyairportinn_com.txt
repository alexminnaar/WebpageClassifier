Located in the heart of Houston - Texas (TX) on famous Airport Boulevard. We strive to give you the best value combined with the best location. You'll find all the comforts of home in our tastefully decorated and spacious rooms, and you'll never find yourself more than a very short distance from any of Houston's major attractions. This hotel in Houston, Texas is less than one mile from the William P. Hobby Airport and offers free shuttle service to Hobby. The hotel features an outdoor pool and all rooms include expanded TV and free Wi-Fi. Rooms at our hotel are furnished with a microwave and a refrigerator. Solid oak furniture and a serene colour palette are showcased in rooms. Downtown Houston is 12.5 miles from the hotel. Our hotel is 13.5 miles from Houston Zoo.
