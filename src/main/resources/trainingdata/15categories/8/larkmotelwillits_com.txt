Convenient Location When driving through beautiful Northern California on scenic US101, you will find the The Lark Motel on South Main Street (US 101 and Route 20.) Room Features All of the clean and comfortable hotel rooms at the Lark Motel have Complimentary Wireless Internet, coffee in the room, AC, Hairdryer, and Cable TV with HBO. Some rooms have mini refrigerators and smoking rooms are available. Great Value! Our rates are always affordable and children under 10 always stay free at the Lark Motel. We take pride in our competitive rates and friendly professional service. If you are looking for comfortable and affordable basic hotel/motel accommodations for your visit to the redwoods area of Northern California, look no further than the Lark Motel.
