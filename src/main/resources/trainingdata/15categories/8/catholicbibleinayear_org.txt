Many Catholics are interested in expanding their Bible knowledge, but need a workable strategy to make this a reality. Others have started, and just need some encouragement along the way. We are making use of a wonderful resource called My Daily Catholic Bible. It is a lot like the “One Year” type Bibles that are so popular in the Protestant world. My Daily Catholic Bible provides an Old Testament and a New Testament reading for each day. In only about 20 minutes daily you can complete the reading of the entire Bible in a year!
