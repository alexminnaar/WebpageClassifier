The hotel is just one block away from Main Street and easily accessible to the 7-subway line, which provides easy access to Manhattan, in addition to buses, express mini-buses, and the Long Island Railroad. Besides being minutes away from both JFK and LaGuardia airport, the Flushing Regent Hotel is also within walking distance of the new Citi Field, the renowned USTA National Tennis Center, the Flushing Meadows-Corona Park, the New York Hall of Science, and the Queens Botanical Gardens. Competitive Rates The Flushing Regent Hotel is located at 134-32 35th Ave in downtown Flushing. The hotel offers spacious accommodations at affordable rates, making it a great alternative to the pricier Manhattan hotels with modestly-sized rooms.
