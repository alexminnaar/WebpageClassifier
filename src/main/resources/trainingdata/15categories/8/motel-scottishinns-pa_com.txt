We offer a comfortable atmosphere of a small motel surrounded by natural beauty. Our standard set of amenities include a microwave, a refrigerator, free Wi-Fi and cable TV (including ShowTime). A work desk is also provided in every guest room. Love’s gas station is only one mile down the road. Haag’s Hotel Restaurant and Blue Mountain Family Restaurant are walking distance from our motel.
