Air Drying Verses Blow Drying There are a lot of myths about whether using a hair dryer is good or bad. You've probably been told that the heat is bad for your hair and it's better to allow it to dry naturally. In truth, even though excess heat is bad for your hair, so is being left wet for extended periods. Wet hair swells and this puts pressure on delicate hair proteins. If you're allowing long, thick hair to take a couple of of hours to dry in a cool room, you'll do more damage to it than you will by using your hair dryer correctly.
