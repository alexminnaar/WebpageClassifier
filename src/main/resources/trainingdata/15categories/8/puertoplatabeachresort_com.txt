The property maintains 23 colonial style residential buildings with 216 rooms, including many with ocean views. The hotel amenities includes a large pool with terrace, jacuzzi, tennis courts, cafeteria, bar, and spa. We our especially proud of our lush tropical landscaping which offers tranquility as well as privacy to our guests. The exquisite scent of Ylang-Ylang at dusk is a truly special treat that our guests look forward to in the evening. There is plenty of parking and 24 hour security and reception services. We are the only hotel located on the Malecon – the lively and beautiful ocean-front drive that runs through downtown Puerto Plata.
