Silverton Inn & Hostel is on the famous Blair Street. Originally an old mining town, Silverton still has it’s small town charm. The entire town is a National Historic Landmark attracting travelers from all over the world from historians to skiers, outdoor enthusiasts and even the train buffs. The historic town is nestled in the San Juan mountain valley with snow capped peaks, beautiful views, hiking, riding the Durango & Silverton Narrow Gauge Steam Train, backcountry skiing, 4WD trails, hunting, fishing, shopping, & exploring old mining towns.
