Ginsey Sesame Street Framed Soft Potty Seat This cushy potty seat features plenty of Sesame Street friends to keep your little one company during potty training. The removable cushion is soft yet durable and very easy to clean. Built-in easy-grip handles add an extra measure of safety and security. The handles also reduce spreading germs by preventing your toddler from grabbing the toilet for balance. Equipped with a built-in deflector to help little guys with their aim. Fits most standard and elongated toilets. The sturdy saddle is constructed of polypropylene and the cushion is constructed of PVC with polyurethane foam padding. Wash with mild soap and water. Measures 11 3/4" x 11" x 2 1/2". Ages 24 months to 5 years. Made in the USA.
