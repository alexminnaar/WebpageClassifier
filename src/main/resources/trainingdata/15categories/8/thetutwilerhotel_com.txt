Experience traditional southern elegance with up-to-date convenience in the heart of downtown at Hampton Inn & Suites Birmingham-Downtown-Tutwiler. The Tutwiler has hosted dignitaries visiting Birmingham since 1914. Start your tour with the lobby’s historic photographs and the original marble and decorative ceilings. Banking and legal offices are steps from the hotel. Explore Birmingham’s culinary scene from four-star restaurants to the popular southern meal known as ‘meat and three.’ Tour the Civil Rights Institute and the Sixteenth Street Baptist Church, a central landmark of the civil rights movement. See sweeping views of the city from ten-acre Vulcan Park featuring the world’s largest cast iron statue. Visit the McWane Science Center, a four-story interactive museum with IMAX theater. Your tour of historic Birmingham continues in your guest room or suite. Room signs feature iconic images of Magic City, artwork was chosen from the archives of the Birmingham Public Library. Unwind in your room with a 40-inch LCD TV or finish a presentation at the work desk. Reserve a one-bedroom suite with amenities such as a fireplace or balcony overlooking downtown.
