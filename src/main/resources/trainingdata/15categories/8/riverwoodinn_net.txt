We are a Motel & RV Park located alongside the Gunnison River. We proudly offer 14 spacious and well-maintained rooms each including microwaves, refrigerators, coffee pots, hair dryers, cable tv, HBO, wireless internet access, and free local calls. Please click here to see our rooms, we have Kitchenette Suites, Queen Rooms, King Rooms, and Double Queen Rooms available. We have ample on-site parking. We are a one story Motel, with interior corridors. The rooms are large and quiet, each room with one bed has a pull-out couch available. We offer a free deluxe continental breakfast served in the mornings. Also we offer a RV park campground with 30 and 50 amp full hook up sites. The park amenities include on site men and women shower-houses, guest coin laundry room, extended cable tv (w/ HBO), and a private back campground with ponds and river access for guests only.
