Give me a Second - Second opinions are good for your health Give Me A Second Mission For women faced with surgery such as hysterectomy, Give Me a Second promotes second opinions to strengthen the doctor - patient partnership, to increase awareness of minimally invasive surgical procedures and to instill confidence in womens' health decisions.
