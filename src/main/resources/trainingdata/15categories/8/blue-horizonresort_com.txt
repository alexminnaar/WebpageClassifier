Blue Horizon Blue Horizon ↓ Skip to Main Content Blue Horizon Menu Welcome to the Blue Horizon - Holiday Resort - Blue Horizon Resort is a spacious family resort attractively perched on Jacamar Hill, just above the world famous Mount Irvine Golf Course. Our resort consist of a swimming pool and 14 self contained apartments in one and/or two bedroom choices. In each of our apartments, we provide separate Kitchen, Living room, Toilet and Bath, Bedroom with or without Lofts, front and back Patios as well as Air conditioning, Satellite T.V., and Telephone service. We offer a sanctuary in the Caribbean southern most Island. Ours is a quiet setting with a panoramic view of Tobago's world famous Bucco Reef and Mount Irvine Golf Course and Bay. We cater to families, couples and guests seeking rest, relaxation and simple island fun. Our reservation office can arrange all island tours, sightseeing, diving, sea-sport, nature trail expeditions, bird watching, local tourist theater or simple room service with your personal maid, cook and guide.
