The new MasterCraft XT22 features a larger cabin and sizeable wake for more comfort and fun on the water, with seating for up to 16 passengers. The 22-foot boat’s all-new hull and MasterCraft’s optional Gen 2 Surf System and hard-sided ballast tanks that accommodate up to 2,700 pounds of ballast are said to create three zones of “perfectly sculpted, sizable waves.” The XT22 can additionally be outfitted with the company’s DockStar Handling System. Mastercraft XT22 top view The MasterCraft XT22 also debuts new customizable Klipsch Audio systems, and a new easy-folding, gas shock assisted ZFT2 tower as new standard equipment. The XT22 comes standard with an Ilmor 5.7L multi-port injection V8 engine with two other Ilmor Gen Five gas engines available.
