Helen is a Bavarian Style Village located in the beautiful North Georgia Mountains. It is the third most visited city in Georgia. It is surrounded by the Blue Ridge Mountains and the famous Chattahoochee River flows through the main part of the city. Oktoberfest begins in early September each year and runs through the end of October. Helen's Oktoberfest draws over 100,000 visitors each year. Other events and festivals occur throughout the year. Other nearby attractions include a championship golf course at Innsbruck, trout fishing, waterfalls, mountain biking, hiking trails.
