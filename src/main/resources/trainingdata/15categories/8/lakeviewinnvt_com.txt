Lake View Inn | Greensboro, Vermont | www.lakeviewinnvt.com Lake View Inn | Greensboro, Vermont | www.lakeviewinnvt.com ↓ Skip to Main Content Lake View Inn VT Welcome to The Lakeview Inn The Lakeview Inn features 10 beautifully appointed guest rooms The Lakeview Inn supports Vermont’s working landscape by providing a study center to promote learning about and the enjoyment of local and regional food products produced by small scale food entrepreneurs. We offer residential learning opportunities to interested individuals to promote awareness, consumption, and production of food from small scale enterprises in Vermont and other rural landscapes. We house student interns conducting experiential learning at local agribusinesses. We welcome writers, artists and musicians for short term residential stays. We also host special events including weddings, reunions, retreats and conferences. Catering services for weddings and other special events are provided by Charlie Hays of Global Bite. Sample menus are available upon request. We look forward to serving your needs and to working with our colleagues in the beautiful Greensboro area.
